About Dr. Carter

Dedicated to Excellence in Dermatology

Professional Background

Dr. Westley Carter is a dermatologist and the founder of Carter Dermatology. Before pursuing medicine, Dr. Carter served our country with distinction as a Lieutenant Colonel in the United States/Oklahoma Army National Guard.

Military Service

He completed two combat tours in Iraq, serving as a physician assistant and earning the Bronze Star Medal. After a 29-year military career, he transitioned to dermatology with the same dedication to service.

Medical Education

He earned his Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine from Lake Erie College of Osteopathic Medicine and completed his dermatology residency at Northeast Regional Medical Center in Kirksville, Missouri.

Advanced Training

Dr. Carter is currently pursuing board certification in Mohs micrographic surgery to further his expertise in skin cancer treatment, ensuring the most precise and effective care for his patients.
